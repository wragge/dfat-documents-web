Cablegram unnumbered 10 November 1939,

Following from Chairman, Wool Committee. [1] Matter price neutrals now extremely urgent also export price of tops. Japan and Belgium pressing strongly for supplies. When do you expect definite answer? Alternatively what is progress to date.

 _

1 Alexander F. Bell.
