Paris, 13 March 1972

1124.Secret

 ****

China

There is still no reply from the PRC Ambassador[1](#f1) although he had today 13 March his first meeting with the American Ambassador. I am beginning to wonder whether in fact there will ever be a reply other than what may be this indirect negative.

2\. Should I keep on seeking an appointment?

Renouf.

[NAA: A1838, 3107/38/18/2, i]

1 Arthur K. Watson.
