Minute 358 MELBOURNE, 19 June 1940

AUSTRALIAN LEGATION AT TOKYO

War Cabinet was of the view that immediate action should be taken to arrange an Australian Legation at Tokyo. The importance was emphasized of taking this step before the international situation deteriorated further to the disadvantage of the British Empire.
