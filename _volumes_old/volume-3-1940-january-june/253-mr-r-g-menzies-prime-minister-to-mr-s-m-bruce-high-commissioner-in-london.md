Cablegram unnumbered 18 May 1940,

MOST SECRET

With reference to Dominions Office telegram 155 regarding Convoy U.S.3 [1] my telegram raising queries [2] was result of sharp division opinion in War Cabinet.

In view answer now received [3] I am strongly advising Cabinet to agree United Kingdom as ultimate destination but cannot arrange Cabinet Meeting until Tuesday. [4] Understand meanwhile Convoy en route Cape Town.

Please convey foregoing unofficially to United Kingdom Government.

MENZIES
