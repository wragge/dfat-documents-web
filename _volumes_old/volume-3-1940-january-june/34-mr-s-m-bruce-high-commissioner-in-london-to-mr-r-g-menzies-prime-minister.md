Cablegram 72 LONDON, 26 January 1940, 7.06 p.m.

MOST SECRET FOR THE PRIME MINISTER PERSONAL FOR HIMSELF ONLY

My telegram No. 71. [1] In my view the proposed Narvik action was an ill-considered stunt for which Winston Churchill [2] responsible.

BRUCE

 _

1 Document 33.

2 U.K. First Lord of the Admiralty.

_
