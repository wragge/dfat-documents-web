Cablegram 15 26 January 1940,

MOST SECRET FOLLOWING FOR BRUCE

Your 63 of 24th January. [1] Scandinavian proposals. Cabinet discussed question generally yesterday. [2] We have received D.38 [3] stating the question involved but not the review requested by you. We assume that no definite action will be decided on by United Kingdom Government until we have had opportunity of examining this and submitting our views.

MENZIES

1 Document 25.

2 See Document 26.

3 Document 24.
