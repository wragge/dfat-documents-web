Minutes 397 and 398 MELBOURNE, 26 June 1940

397\. Appointment of Australian Minister in Tokio: Agreed to approach Sir John Latham [1] to ascertain whether he would accept the post.

398\. Agreed to announce that it is proposed to establish a Legation in China simultaneously with the appointment to Tokio.

 _

1 Chief Justice of the High Court of Australia.
