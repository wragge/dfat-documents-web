Cablegram 146 LONDON, 26 February 1940, 7.26 p.m.

MOST IMMEDIATE MOST SECRET FOR PRIME MINISTER PERSONAL

My telegram 24th February. [1] War Cabinet divided and Prime Minister [2] has postponed further consideration pending the receipt of reactions of Dominions. Most desirable that your views should be received at the earliest possible moment.

 _

1 Document 76.

2 Neville Chamberlain.

_
