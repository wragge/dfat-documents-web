Cablegram UN954, NEW YORK, 5 December 1949, 5.25 p.m.

China Item.

Cuba, Ecuador and Peru today proposed reference of China question to the Interim Committee. U.S.A., Australia and Mexico spoke in opposition. Vote tomorrow.
