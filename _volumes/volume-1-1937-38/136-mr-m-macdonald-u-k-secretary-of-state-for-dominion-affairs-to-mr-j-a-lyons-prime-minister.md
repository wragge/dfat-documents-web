Cablegram 29 LONDON, 14 March 1938, 8.07 p.m.

CONFIDENTIAL

Following for Prime Minister:-

Your telegram of 12th March, No. 25. [1] Your action much appreciated. In this connection please see my telegram of today, Circular B. No. 55. [2]

 _

1 Document 135.

2 Not printed. It said that it had been made clear to the United Kingdom press that it remained desirable that they 'should refrain from nagging and provocation and publication of unconfirmed news'.
