# 268 Lt Col W. R. Hodgson, Secretary of Department of External Affairs, to Mr A. T. Stirling, External Affairs Officer in London

## Cablegram 55 21 September 1938,

Your 141 9th September. [1] This information still regarded most important by Government, but we have not had it confirmed any other source. Do you know if this attitude still holds.

HODGSON
