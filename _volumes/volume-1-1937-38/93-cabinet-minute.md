# 93 Cabinet Minute

## Minute 2241 MELBOURNE, 1 November 1937

COMMONWEALTH COMMISSIONER IN JAPAN

Decided Longfield Lloyd at present Trade Commissioner in Japan to be called Commonwealth Commissioner. [1]
