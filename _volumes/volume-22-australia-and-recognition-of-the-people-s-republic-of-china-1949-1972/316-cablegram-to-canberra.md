# 316 CABLEGRAM TO CANBERRA

Paris, 10 March 1972

1069.Secret

****

My telegram 1053:1 China

I am still unable to get an answer from the Chinese Ambassador so I guess he is awaiting instructions.

I am continuing to press.

Renouf.

[ [NAA: A1838, 3107/38/18/2, i](http://www.naa.gov.au/cgi-bin/Search?O=I&Number=1730901) ]

1 9 March. Renouf reported that he had asked for an appointment with the Chinese Ambassador and as yet had received no reply.
