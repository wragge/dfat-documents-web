# 348 Mr John Curtin, Prime Minister, to Sir Earle Page, Special Representative in the United Kingdom

Cablegram 30 CANBERRA, 20 February 1942

MOST IMMEDIATE MOST SECRET

Reference Your P.47. [1] If you have not done so you should act at once on cablegrams Nos. 28 [2] and 29. [3] In No. 27 [4] you were asked, pending receipt of decision in No. 28, to ensure that convoy should not be committed to Burma.

CURTIN

_

1 Document 347 2 Document 345.

3 Dispatched 19 February. On file AA:A816, 52/302/142. It amplified the decision published in Document 345.

4 Document 343.
