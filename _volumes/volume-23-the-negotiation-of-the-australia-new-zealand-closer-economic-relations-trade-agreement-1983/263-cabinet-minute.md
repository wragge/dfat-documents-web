Wellington, [14] February 198[3]

CM 83/4/1. CONFIDENTIAL

 **CER: Signature of Agreement**

At the meeting on 14 February 1983 Cabinet authorised New Zealand's signature of the Australia New Zealand Closer Economic Relations-Trade Agreement, 1983, the Agreement to take effect from 1 January 1983.

CS (83) 56 refers.
