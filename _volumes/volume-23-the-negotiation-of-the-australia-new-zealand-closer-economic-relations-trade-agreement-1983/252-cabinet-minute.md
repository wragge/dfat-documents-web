# 252 Cabinet Minute

Wellington, 24 August 1982

CM 82/3417. CONFIDENTIAL

**Proposed Closer Economic Relationship Between Australia and New Zealand (ANZCER): Outcome of the Consultations**

At the meeting on 23 August 1982, on the recommendation of the Cabinet Economic Committee, Cabinet _approved_ the initialling of the Draft Heads of Agreement _subject to_ the outcome of the consultation on the CER proposal in Australia and the satisfactory resolution of outstanding intermediate goods issues affecting whiteware and carpets.
