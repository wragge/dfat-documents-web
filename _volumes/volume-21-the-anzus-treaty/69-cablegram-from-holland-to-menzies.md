# 69 Cablegram From Holland to Menzies

Cablegram, Wellington, 20 March 1951

37\. IMMEDIATE TOP SECRET

Thank you for your personal message[1] on the Pacific Pact. I entirely share the views you express. We have greatly welcomed the opportunity of discussing the whole matter with Mr. Watt, who is taking back with him the draft communication which I would like to send to Mr. Attlee.[2]

We would be grateful for any comments and views you may care to express.

_ 1 Document 63.

2 See Document 68.
