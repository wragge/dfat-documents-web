Cablegram 150 LONDON, 28 February 1940, 6.20 p.m.

MOST SECRET FOR THE PRIME MINISTER

Norwegian territorial waters. Discussion postponed till tomorrow to allow time for consideration of cables from you [1] and Smuts.
