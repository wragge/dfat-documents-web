# 411 Mr R. G. Menzies, Prime Minister, to Mr R. G. Casey, Minister to the United States

Cablegram unnumbered 20 June 1940,

PERSONAL

Suggest that arrival of Australian troops in Great Britain may provide you with theme well worth emphasizing at the present time.

MENZIES
