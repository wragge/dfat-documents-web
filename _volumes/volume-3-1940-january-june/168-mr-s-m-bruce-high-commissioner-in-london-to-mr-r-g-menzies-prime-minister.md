# 168 Mr S. M. Bruce, High Commissioner in London, to Mr R. G. Menzies, Prime Minister

Cablegram 263 LONDON, 22 April 1940

FOR THE PRIME MINISTER PERSONAL HIMSELF ONLY

Reference suggested Prime Ministers meeting. I feel strongly imperative such meeting should be held and while recognising your local difficulties sincerely trust you will find it possible to attend.

BRUCE
