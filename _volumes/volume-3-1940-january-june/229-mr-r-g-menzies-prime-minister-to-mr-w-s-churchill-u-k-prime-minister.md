# 229 Mr R. G. Menzies, Prime Minister, to Mr W. S. Churchill, U.K. Prime Minister

## Cablegram unnumbered 11 May 1940,

My colleagues and I congratulate you on your appointment as Prime Minister. We wish you the fullest measure of success. I need hardly assure you that Australians will co-operate to the utmost in the war effort directed by your Government.

MENZIES
