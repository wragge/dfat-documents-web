Cablegram unnumbered LONDON, 18 April 1940, 4.10 a.m.

PERSONAL AND MOST SECRET

Your telegrams 153 and 155 [1]: I am most grateful to you for communicating your views so promptly on this important question of policy. I am bringing them urgently before the War Cabinet. I shall communicate with you again as soon as possible, but should like you meanwhile to have this personal message.

 _

1 Documents 148 and 151.
