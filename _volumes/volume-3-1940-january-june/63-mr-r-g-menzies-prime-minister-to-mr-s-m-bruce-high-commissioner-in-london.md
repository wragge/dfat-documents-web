Cablegram unnumbered 17 February 1940,

MOST SECRET PERSONAL HIMSELF ONLY

Thanks your survey 13th February. [1] We will be guided by Chiefs of Staff in what theatre of war troops are used but must insist that training be adequate except possibly under conditions of emergency.

MENZIES
