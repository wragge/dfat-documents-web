# 349 General J. C. Smuts, South African Prime Minister, to Mr R. G. Menzies, Prime Minister

Cablegram 15 PRETORIA, 7 June 1940, 2.24 p.m.

MOST SECRET

Thank you for your message of 6th June. [1] Although I was doubtful at the time about the policy of your message to the President [2], I feel that the valuable response has justified your action.

_

1 Not found. It presumably contained Roosevelt's message printed as Document 326.

2 See Document 280.
