# 395 General J. C. Smuts, South African Prime Minister, to Mr R. G. Menzies, Prime Minister

Cablegram 17 PRETORIA, 17 June 1940, 12.45 p.m.

MOST SECRET

Your telegram 15th June. [1]

I have asked Churchill [2] whether any further communications from the Dominions to Roosevelt are suggested either separately or collectively. No answer yet.

_

1 See Document 380, note 5.

2 U.K. Prime Minister.
