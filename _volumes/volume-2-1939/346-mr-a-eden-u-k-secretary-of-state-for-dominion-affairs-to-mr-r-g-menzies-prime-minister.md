# 346 Mr A. Eden, U.K. Secretary of State for Dominion Affairs, to Mr R.G. Menzies, Prime Minister

Circular Cablegram D75 LONDON, 10 November 1939, 1.54 p.m.

MOST IMMEDIATE MOST SECRET

My circular telegram D.73 of today. [1] Following for Prime Minister.

The text of the draft reply follows in my immediately following telegram. [2] Please reply not later than 6 p.m. London time tomorrow Saturday 11th November whether you concur in the text of the draft and the procedure outlined in my telegram referred to above.

The text of the draft reply is also being communicated to the French Government and the Polish Government.
