# 293 Mr A. Eden, U.K. Secretary of State for Dominion Affairs, to Mr R.G. Menzies, Prime Minister

Cablegram 135 LONDON, 13 October 1939

SECRET

Your telegram 13 9. [1]

We shall be delighted to welcome Casey to this country again [2] , and look forward to seeing him. We are also glad to learn that it has been possible to spare so strong a team of advisers.

_

1 Document 291.

2 R.G. Casey, Minister for Supply and Development, who had been Commonwealth Government Liaison Officer in London 1924-27 and 1927-31.
