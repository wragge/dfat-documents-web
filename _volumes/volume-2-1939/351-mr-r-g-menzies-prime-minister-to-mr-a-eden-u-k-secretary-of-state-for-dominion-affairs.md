Cablegram 162 11 November 1939,

Reference suggested reply to the Queen of the Netherlands. [1] We agree with its terms and are willing that our advice to that effect should be referred to in the reply. [2]

MENZIES

 _

1 See Document 347.

2 This cablegram was repeated to the Canadian, New Zealand and South African Prime Ministers.
