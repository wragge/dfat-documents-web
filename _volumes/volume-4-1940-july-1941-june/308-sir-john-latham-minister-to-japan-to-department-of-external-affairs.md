# 308 Sir John Latham, Minister to Japan, to Department of External Affairs

## Cablegram 93 TOKYO, 19 February 1941, 6.35 p.m.

[Repeated to] Washington 16, London 18.

My telegram No. 87. [1] Defensive measures in Malaya and reports of concern with respect to Eastern situation shown in London Washington and Australia have had steadying effect here.

LATHAM
